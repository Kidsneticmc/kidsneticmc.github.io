Program Overview

Kidsnetic Mindfulness Club is a structured, school-based after-school enrichment program serving elementary-aged children in Austin, Texas. The program supports whole-child development through mindfulness, social-emotional learning (SEL), creative art, movement, and cooperative group games.

Program Focus

  • Mindfulness and emotional regulation
  • Social-emotional learning (SEL)
  • Creative art projects
  • Yoga, movement, and structured group games
  • Small-group and whole-group activities

How the Program Operates

  • School-based programming on elementary campuses
  • Structured weekly lesson plans and activity stations
  • Supervised student groups led by trained staff
  • Consistent daily schedules and expectations

Privacy Statement

Kidsnetic Mindfulness Club intentionally limits public-facing marketing and does not maintain business social media accounts. As a program serving elementary-aged children, we prioritize student privacy and operate primarily through direct school partnerships and internal communication.
