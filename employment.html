Employment Opportunities

Kidsnetic Mindfulness Club employs part-time after-school staff to support daily programming with elementary-aged children in a school-based setting. Positions are well suited for individuals interested in education, child development, psychology, counseling, or related fields.

Typical Schedule

  • Monday–Friday after school
  • Approximate hours: 2:45 PM – 6:00 PM
  • School-year positions

Responsibilities

  • Support mindfulness and social-emotional learning activities
  • Facilitate art, movement, and cooperative group games
  • Supervise elementary-aged children
  • Assist with structured lesson plans and activity stations
  • Maintain a safe, positive learning environment
