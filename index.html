After-School Enrichment That Builds Calm, Confidence & Connection

Kidsnetic Mindfulness Club is a structured, on-campus after-school enrichment program located at Brentwood Elementary in Austin, Texas, serving elementary-aged children (K–5). The program provides a consistent daily routine that integrates mindfulness practices, social-emotional learning (SEL), creative art, movement, and collaborative group games.
